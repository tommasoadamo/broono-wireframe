⑥ THE SCIENCE
THE FORMULATION RATIONALE
Why this formula. Why these doses.
The five systems that determine how a dog ages — immune, antioxidant, metabolic, musculoskeletal, and digestive — are not independent. They interact. A formulation that addresses only one system in isolation misses the compounding effect of supporting them together. Essential is built around that interaction. Every ingredient was selected for its specific mechanism and dosed to the level studied in the research, not the minimum required to appear on a label.
No proprietary blends. No vague claims. Every ingredient listed, every dose declared, every mechanism sourced. Because the people who choose Broono already know how to read a label.

⑩ FAQ
Frequently asked
How long until I see results? +
Structural outcomes take time. Most pet parents start noticing changes — in coat condition, energy levels, or mobility — at 4–8 weeks of consistent daily use. The supplement is building a biological baseline, not producing an immediate effect. Consistency is the mechanism.
Is Essential suitable for all dogs? +
Essential is formulated for dogs of all ages and breeds. If your dog has a diagnosed health condition or is on medication, we recommend checking with your vet before starting any supplement.
Can I give Essential alongside a Targeted Care product? +
Yes. Essential is designed as the daily foundation; Targeted Care products (Move, Prebiotic, Calm) address specific systems on top of that baseline. The two collections are designed to work together.
How does the subscription work? +
You choose the delivery frequency that matches your dog's weight and usage. You can pause, change frequency, or cancel at any time from your account — no fees, no lock-in. The subscription saves 20% versus one-time purchase.
Where is Essential made? +
Made in Italy in our family laboratory, to pharmaceutical-grade manufacturing standards (ISO 22000). Every incoming ingredient and every finished batch is independently tested before leaving the facility.
