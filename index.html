⑨b DOG AGING PROJECT

50,000 dogs are teaching scientists how to age well. We read every paper.

The Dog Aging Project is the largest open-science study of ageing ever conducted on companion animals. What it's finding is changing how veterinary nutritionists think about daily care — which five systems determine how well a dog ages, which daily inputs move those systems, and how much of a dog's healthspan is within an owner's control. We built Broono around those findings.
